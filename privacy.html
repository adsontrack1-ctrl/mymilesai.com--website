Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026 · Effective immediately

📋 Summary: We collect only the data needed to track your mileage. We never sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

MyMilesAI ("we", "our", or "us") is a mileage tracking application accessible at mymilesai.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use MyMilesAI:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo from Google Sign-In
  • Location data: GPS coordinates while the app is active and you are driving
  • Trip data: Start/end addresses, distances, timestamps, and categories you assign
  • Device information: Browser type and device type for app compatibility
  • Usage data: App settings and preferences you configure

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Record and display your mileage trips
  • Calculate tax deductions based on IRS mileage rates
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Generate mileage reports for export
  • Improve app performance and fix issues

4. Location Data

Location data is the core of our service. Here's exactly how we handle it:

  • GPS is only active when you have the app open and GPS is enabled
  • Location coordinates are used to calculate distance traveled
  • Coordinates are converted to readable addresses using OpenStreetMap (Nominatim)
  • Raw GPS coordinates are not stored permanently — only trip start/end addresses and total miles
  • We never track your location in the background without your knowledge

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which provides:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Access limited to your account only — no other user can see your trips
  • Data stored on Google's secure cloud infrastructure

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • OpenStreetMap/Nominatim: For address lookup (anonymous queries only)
  • ipapi.co: For detecting your country to filter address search results (anonymous)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data in the app at any time
  • Export: Download your trip data as CSV or PDF
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all data via Settings → Legal → Delete Account
  • Correct: Edit any trip data directly in the app

8. Children's Privacy

MyMilesAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
